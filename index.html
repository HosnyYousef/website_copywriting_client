01

Sourced before it’s written

Every factual item carries its source in the plan itself, including the Arab Monetary Fund, Islamic Finance News, and Islamic Finance Gateway, so nothing reaches a client feed unverified.

02

Written once, cut per platform

The long-form post comes first, followed by a hand-trimmed Twitter version that keeps the figure, hashtag, and member-country reference while dropping secondary clauses.

03

Bilingual in the same document

Keeping Arabic and English side by side in one plan, rather than translating after the fact, keeps tone and terminology consistent across both audiences.

04

A varied weekly rhythm

Across the week, I balance news, thought leadership, explainers, and values-based content so the feed never reads as a single repeated note.

01

Sourced before it’s written

Every factual item carries its origin in the plan itself — the Arab Monetary Fund, Islamic Finance News, Islamic Finance Gateway — so nothing reaches a client feed unverified.

02

Written once, cut per platform

The long-form post is drafted first, then trimmed to Twitter’s limit by hand — keeping the figure, the hashtag, and the member-country reference while dropping secondary clauses.

03

Bilingual in the same document

Arabic and English sit side by side in one plan rather than being translated after the fact, so tone and terminology stay consistent across both audiences.

04

A varied weekly rhythm

News, thought leadership, explainers, and values-based content are balanced across the week so the feed never reads as a single repeated note.
